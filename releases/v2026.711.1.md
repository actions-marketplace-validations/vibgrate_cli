# Vibgrate CLI 2026.711.1

_Released 2026-07-11_

This release of the Vibgrate CLI includes the addition of documentation related to the scoring methodology. Users can now access the public scoring specification, the risk and drift scoring whitepaper, and shared legal notes directly from the repository.

## What changed

### New

- Published the scoring methodology documentation in the repository, including the public scoring specification, risk and drift scoring whitepaper, and shared legal notes.
